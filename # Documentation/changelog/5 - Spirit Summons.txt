#------------------------------------------
# Changes: Spirit Summons
#------------------------------------------
- Reduced Spirit Summon FP costs.
- Removed Spirit Summon HP costs.
- Ancestral Follower: now spawns 2 Ancestral Followers.

- Condensed all Grave Glovewort items into one item: Grave Glovewort 
- Condensed all Ghost Glovewort items into one item: Ghost Glovewort 

- Spirit Summon Ashes can no longer be reinforced.
 - They have the following modifiers:
  - HP: *2 
  - Max Stamina: *2
  - Defence %: *2
  - Resist %: *2
  - Power %: *2 (*1 for Mimic)
  
- Spirit Summon Ashes are now consumable.

- Roderika now sells Spirit Summon Ashes in exchange for Grave or Ghost Glovewort, plus runes. 
 - Once you find the initial item, they will then appear in the shop.
