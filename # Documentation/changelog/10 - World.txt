#------------------------------------------
# Changes: World
#------------------------------------------
# Torrent
- Torrent can be mounted anywhere.
- Added spiritspring in Southwest Liurnia: allows access to Moonlight Altar area.

# Scaling
- Enemy physical defence is now *2 to account for higher early base damage.
- Enemy elemental defence is now *1.5 to account for higher early base damage.
- Enemy HP in the areas before Mt. Gelmir is now higher to account for higher early base damage.

# Map
- The door to the lower section of Roundtable Hold from the staircase near Hewg can now be opened (from Alberich's side) after Mad Tongue Alberich has been killed.
 - The warp beacon has been removed since it is no longer needed.
