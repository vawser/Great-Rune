# General
- Added explicit values in the descriptions for talismans.

# Weapons
- Sacred weapons will now deal 5% additional damage to Undead enemies and prevent resurrection.
- Standard Reinforcement path now only takes 1 Smithing Stone for each level (was 2/4/6).

# Armor
- Added weak stagger resistance for armor chestpieces that weigh between 10.0 and 14.9.
- Added medium stagger resistance for armor chestpieces that weigh between 15.0 and 19.9.
- Added strong stagger resistance for armor chestpieces that weigh between 20.0 and above.

# Spirit Summons
- Mimic Tear: reduced damage multiplier from reinforcement to {1 -> 1.5} (was {1 -> 2.5}).
- Mimic Tear: reduced HP multiplier from reinforcement to {1 -> 2} (was {1 -> 3.7}).
- Reduced the poise of some of the spirit summons so they stagger more often.

# Area Scaling
- Reduced the HP multiplier for each tier of area scaling. Now ranges from *1 to *3.3 (was *1 to *7.9).
- Increased baseline rune gain for each tier of area scaling to 2.0 (was 1.0).
- Reduced the damage multiplier for each tier of area scaling. Now ranges from *1 to *2.8 (was *1 to *3.7).
